
Ik heb nogal moeite met het echte rappoort gedeelte maar hier zijn in ieder geval een aantal punten waarmee de site "malpieheide.nl" verbetered kan worden.

1. -establish a linkbuilding technique, the page that links back to your content should be related to your site. (forexample a link from a vet would be worth more than that of a bank. because it's directly related to the topic of dogs).
2. -add your site to a directory or resource page associated with your content
3. -add more focus on unique Long tail searches and on local SEO
4. -Link from high authority domain (Make sure it's not an unnatural link)
5. -add 301 redirects to fix the traffic between the regular and sub-domain.
6. -The links position on a page is important, keep this in mind when linkbuilding.
7. -Make sure the link and the actual content are relevant to eachother and not just keyword/anchor text spam.
8. -Mobile optimisation is a large influence in ranking factor.
